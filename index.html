About Us

We Are MHS

At MHS, we transform agricultural supply chains from local to global markets. Our goal is to empower small-scale players by offering access to capital and structured support. We address challenges like limited resources and market access, building robust supply chains that integrate every player, from breeders to buyers, into the global market.

Our solutions include Embedded Trade Finance to streamline trade processes and ease financial burdens. We use Technology like AI, ML, and blockchain for efficient data management and transactions. Our Payment Channels offer flexible options such as Mobile Money and Escrow Services. Additionally, our B2B Commodity Marketplace ensures market readiness with services like cleaning and packaging, promoting transparency and accessibility in global trade.
